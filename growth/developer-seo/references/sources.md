## Source discipline

- Date any volatile claim.
- Prefer direct observation and first-party documentation.
- State when behavior is eligibility guidance rather than a ranking guarantee.
- Treat patents, quality-rater guidance, leaks, correlation studies, and tool scores as different evidence classes.
- Preserve links and methodology for audits so another practitioner can reproduce the conclusion.
